Hershey’s Chocolate World attraction delivers special edition summer treats

The US Hershey’s Chocolate World Attraction is playing host to a number of exclusive confectionery creations this summer, as the sector continues to recovery amid the ongoing pandemic, reports Neill Barston. According to the business, its Pennsylvania centre has conjured up a host of limited-time treats for its visitors, with the site offering plenty of sweet surprises. Among its special edition items only available at Hershey’s Chocolate World, its latest series includes Hershey’s Ice Cream Shoppe bars, a brand-new Reese’s Stuff Your Cup experience coming soon where guests can personalise a larger-than-life Reese’s Peanut Butter Cup with peanut butter direct from the factory with mix-ins of their choice. Guests can also visit Hershey’s Chocolate World’s iconic attractions including Hershey’s largest candy store, the free Hershey’s Chocolate Tour, chocolate tasting, a Create Your Own Candy Bar factory experience. There are also locations in New York’s Times Square and Las Vegas. We know that families are ready to get out of their homes and reclaim their summer, so that’s why Hershey’s Chocolate World is adding sweetness this year more than ever,” said Todd Kohr, Marketing Manager, Hershey’s Chocolate World Attraction. “From our over-the-top customisable Reese’s Peanut Butter Cup experience coming soon and Custom Creation kits to our limited-time-only milkshake flavours, guests will experience new twists on classic Hershey treats.”

🧁 Cupcakes fun facts:

  • Original cupcake recipes were not frosted, and usually were usually just flavored with spices or dried fruit.
  • Cupcakes gained popularity in 1919 when Hostess became mass producing them. But they weren’t the cream filled, frosted kind we all know until the 1950’s.
  • Cupcakes started gaining popularity in the early 2000’s when NYC shops like Magnolia Bakery were featured on Sex and the City.
  • The record for eating the most cupcakes in the shortest time is 29 cupcakes in 30 seconds.
  • The world’s largest cupcake weighed over 1,200 lbs. and had over 2 million calories.
  • The first “cupcake only” bakery is Sprinkles Cupcakes, opened in 2005. They make over 25,000 cupcakes a day from 11 locations.
